Fonts!
In this mini guide I will share with you safe fonts and colors you can use in your
designs. Let’s start with fonts.
When you’re a junior designer chances are you want to experiment with all the
choices that are out there.
Don’t.
It’s really easy for typography to get out of control, so instead try to use these safe
fonts that are a lot easier to work with:

For your first 3-5 projects pick JUST ONE and avoid font pairing. Yes, your projects will
likely be a tiny bit more boring than if you’d used a different font for headings and
different one for body copy, but at least it will be SAFE!

Colors
Let’s start by defining our text color. The idea is to use a dark grey (not black!) with a
hint of our primary color in it. If your primary color is blue, your dark grey should have
a hint of blue in it. Our labels should be a lighter (but still visible) version of this.
