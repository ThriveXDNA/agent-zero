## **PART 1: FACEBOOK ALGORITHM - UNDERSTANDING WHAT WORKS**

### **1.1 How Facebook\'s Algorithm Actually Works**

Facebook\'s algorithm prioritizes content based on user relationships,
content type, and engagement. The platform has shifted toward video but
maintains unique characteristics compared to other social platforms.

The Core Ranking Factors for Facebook (Ranked by Importance):

\| Priority \| Signal \| Why It Matters \|

\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|

\| 1 (Highest) \| Relationships \| Content from friends/family gets
priority. Brand content must earn engagement to compete. \|

\| 2 \| Engagement \| Likes, comments, shares signal value. Shares are
the strongest signal for reach. \|

\| 3 \| Content Type \| Video (especially Reels) is currently favored.
Links perform poorly. \|

\| 4 \| Timing \| Content posted when audience is active gets initial
boost. \|

\| 5 (Lowest) \| Quality \| Original content is favored over
recycled/duplicated content. \|

The Facebook Reach Reality:

Organic reach on Facebook Pages has declined significantly. To succeed,
you must either:

\- Pay for reach (Facebook Ads)

\- Build a Facebook Group

\- Leverage Reels discovery

\- Have highly engaging content that gets shared

### **1.2 Content Type Performance**

\| Content Type \| Organic Reach \| Best For \|

\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|

\| Facebook Reels \| High (discovery) \| New audience discovery \|

\| Video (native) \| Medium \| Engagement, watch time \|

\| Images \| Low-Medium \| Brand awareness \|

\| Links \| Very Low \| Traffic (but poor reach) \|

\| Text Posts \| Low \| Community building (Groups) \|

\| Stories \| Medium \| Connection with followers \|

## **PART 2: CONTENT FORMAT SPECIFICATIONS**

### **2.1 Facebook Reels Requirements**

\| Specification \| Standard \| Notes \|

\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\--\|

\| Duration \| 30-90 seconds \| Can go up to 10 minutes, but shorter
performs better for discovery \|

\| Aspect Ratio \| 9:16 vertical \| Full screen mobile format \|

\| Resolution \| 1080x1920 minimum \| 4K preferred \|

\| File Format \| MP4 or MOV \| Best compatibility \|

\| Audio \| On by default \| Facebook viewers often have sound on \|

\| Captions \| Required \| Add in CapCut or Facebook\'s built-in
captions \|

\| Trending Audio \| Recommended \| Using trending audio can boost
discovery \|

Reels That Work:

\- Quick tips and hacks

\- Myth-busting content

\- Behind-the-scenes glimpses

\- Educational micro-content

\- Trending sounds with unique angle

### **2.2 Facebook Video Requirements**

\| Specification \| Standard \| Notes \|

\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\--\|

\| Duration \| 3-10 minutes \| Longer videos can work if engaging \|

\| Aspect Ratio \| 16:9 horizontal or 9:16 vertical \| Both work \|

\| Resolution \| 1280x720 minimum \| HD required \|

\| File Format \| MP4 or MOV \| Best compatibility \|

\| Captions \| Required \| Add for accessibility \|

\| Thumbnail \| Custom recommended \| Upload custom thumbnail \|

### **2.3 Image Posts**

\| Specification \| Standard \| Notes \|

\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\--\|

\| Aspect Ratio \| 1:1 (square) or 1.91:1 (landscape) \| 1:1 works best
in feed \|

\| Resolution \| 1200x630 minimum \| High quality required \|

\| File Format \| JPG or PNG \| High quality required \|

\| Text on Images \| Use sparingly \| Facebook limits text on images in
ads \|

### **2.4 Link Posts**

Important: Link posts have very low organic reach on Facebook. Avoid
posting links directly to Facebook. Instead:

\- Post the link preview but add significant value in the caption

\- Share the content as an image instead

\- Drive traffic through Facebook Ads

## **PART 3: POSTING BEST PRACTICES**

### **3.1 Posting Schedule**

Optimal Posting Times:

\- Morning: 7:00 AM - 9:00 AM CT

\- Midday: 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM CT

\- Evening: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM CT

Best Days:

\- Tuesday through Thursday (highest engagement)

\- Monday and Friday (moderate)

\- Weekends (variable)

Posting Frequency:

\- Reels: 1+ per day

\- Videos: 3-5 per week

\- Images/Posts: 3-5 per week

### **3.2 Caption Best Practices**

Caption Structure:

Hook (first line - most important)

\-

Body (value, context, story)

\-

CTA (what you want them to do)

\-

#hashtags (optional - Facebook doesn\'t prioritize hashtags)

Caption Rules:

\- First line must hook (visible before \"See More\")

\- Keep captions conversational

\- Use line breaks for readability

\- Include CTAs

\- Hashtags are optional on Facebook (less important than other
platforms)

### **3.3 Hashtag Strategy**

Hashtag Rules:

\- Use 2-5 hashtags max (optional)

\- Facebook doesn\'t prioritize hashtags like Instagram

\- Focus on 1-2 niche-specific hashtags

\- Don\'t over-tag

## **PART 4: ENGAGEMENT RULES**

### **4.1 Comment Management**

Response Time Requirements:

\- First hour: Critical (Facebook boosts posts with early engagement)

\- Within 4 hours: Respond to all comments

\- Within 24 hours: Reply to every single comment

Why Early Replies Matter:

Facebook\'s algorithm prioritizes content with active engagement. Quick
responses encourage more comments, triggering broader distribution.

Comment Response Guidelines:

\- Respond to questions with helpful answers

\- Ask follow-up questions to spark conversation

\- Thank viewers for specific compliments

\- Never argue with negative commenters---delete or hide

### **4.2 Engagement Strategies**

Within Your Content:

\- Reels/Video: End with question, use text overlays

\- Images: Ask questions in caption

\- Posts: Encourage comments and shares

Outside Your Content:

\- Reply to comments on your posts first

\- Comment on other relevant pages daily

\- Engage with your niche community

\- Respond to messages within 24 hours

### **4.3 Facebook Groups Strategy**

Why Facebook Groups Matter:

\- Higher organic engagement than Pages

\- Build community around your niche

\- Establish authority and trust

\- Direct communication with followers

Group Management:

\- Create a group related to your niche

\- Post valuable content regularly

\- Engage with every member

\- Run polls and discussions

\- Welcome new members personally

## **PART 7: VIDEO PRODUCTION WORKFLOW**

### **7.1 Creating Facebook Reels (Reference to Main Guide)**

For detailed video production steps, see
\`VIDEOPRODUCTIONCHECKLISTWITHWHY.md\`. Below is the abbreviated
workflow for Facebook Reels:

Phase 1: Research (15-30 min)

1\. Check Facebook\'s trending content

2\. Identify what performs well in your niche

3\. Research competitor pages

4\. Validate your topic idea

Phase 2: Concept Development (15 min)

1\. Design your hook (first 1-2 seconds)

2\. Plan the content structure

3\. Write script if needed

4\. Choose trending audio if available

Phase 3: Production (30-60 min)

1\. Record A-roll (on-camera, high energy)

2\. Record B-roll (supporting visuals)

3\. Record multiple takes

Phase 4: Editing (30-60 min)

1\. Cut to optimal length (30-90 seconds)

2\. Add captions (every word)

3\. Add text overlays

4\. Use trending audio if applicable

5\. Export at 1080x1920

Phase 5: Upload & Optimize

1\. Write caption with strong hook

2\. Add 2-5 hashtags (optional)

3\. Include CTA

4\. Post at optimal times
