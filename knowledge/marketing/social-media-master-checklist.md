## **PLATFORM PRIORITY MATRIX**

When time is limited, prioritize in this order:

### **High Priority (Do First)**

1\. Twitter/X: 5-10 tweets/day, fast-moving algorithm

2\. TikTok: 1-3 videos/day, discovery-based

3\. YouTube Shorts: 1-2/day, growth engine

### **Medium Priority**

4\. Instagram Reels: 1/day, discovery

5\. Instagram Stories: 3-5/day, connection

6\. LinkedIn: 3-5 posts/week, professional

### **Lower Priority**

7\. Facebook: 1-2 posts/day, lower organic

8\. YouTube Long-Form: 1/week, authority building

## **MINIMUM DAILY REQUIREMENTS**

## **TROUBLESHOOTING**

### **What to Do If\...**

You run out of time:

\- Prioritize Twitter/X, TikTok, YouTube Shorts

\- Skip Instagram Stories if needed

\- Schedule LinkedIn for tomorrow

Content isn\'t performing:

\- Check hook (first 2 seconds)

\- Review timing

\- Analyze engagement

\- Try different format

Algorithm seems slow:

\- Increase engagement on other accounts

\- Post at optimal times

\- Use trending content

\- Be patient - algorithms vary
