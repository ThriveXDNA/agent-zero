## **PART 1: INSTAGRAM ALGORITHM - UNDERSTANDING WHAT WORKS**

### **1.1 How Instagram\'s Algorithm Actually Works**

Instagram\'s algorithm prioritizes content based on user relationships
and interests. The platform has shifted heavily toward video (Reels) but
carousels and Stories remain powerful for different goals.

The Core Ranking Factors for Instagram (Ranked by Importance):

\| Priority \| Signal \| Why It Matters \|

\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|

\| 1 (Highest) \| Saves \| Someone bookmarked your post = highest
intent. This is the single strongest signal for reach. \|

\| 2 \| Shares \| Someone sent your post to a friend via DM or Story.
This is massive algorithmic signal. \|

\| 3 \| Comments \| Especially comments that spark conversation. Respond
to every comment to boost the post. \|

\| 4 \| Likes \| Positive signal but far less important than
saves/shares. Don\'t chase likes. \|

\| 5 \| Watch Time (Reels) \| How long people watch your Reel.
Completion rate is critical for Reel distribution. \|

\| 6 \| Following \| Content from accounts users follow gets priority,
but reach to non-followers is possible through exploration. \|

The Golden Metric: Saves + Shares

Unlike other platforms that prioritize likes, Instagram\'s algorithm
heavily rewards saves and shares. These signals indicate:

\- Saves: The content is valuable enough to revisit later

\- Shares: The content is worth sending to friends

If people save or share your content, Instagram shows it to thousands
more people.

## **PART 2: CONTENT FORMAT SPECIFICATIONS**

### **2.1 Instagram Reels Requirements**

\| Specification \| Standard \| Notes \|

\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\--\|

\| Duration \| 15-30 seconds (sweet spot) \| Can go up to 90 seconds,
but shorter = higher completion \|

\| Aspect Ratio \| 9:16 vertical \| Full screen mobile format \|

\| Resolution \| 1080x1920 minimum \| 4K preferred \|

\| File Format \| MP4 or MOV \| Best compatibility \|

\| Audio \| On by default \| Instagram viewers expect sound \|

\| Captions \| Required \| Add in CapCut or Instagram\'s built-in
captions \|

\| Trending Audio \| Strongly Recommended \| Using trending audio boosts
discovery significantly \|

Reels That Work:

\- Quick tips and hacks (15-30 seconds)

\- Myth-busting content

\- Behind-the-scenes glimpses

\- Educational micro-content

\- Trending sounds with unique angle

\- Before/after transformations

### **2.2 Instagram Carousel Requirements**

\| Specification \| Standard \| Notes \|

\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\--\|

\| Slides \| 5-10 slides per carousel \| More slides = more time spent =
higher reach \|

\| Aspect Ratio \| 1:1 (square) or 4:5 (portrait) \| 4:5 takes more
screen space \|

\| Resolution \| 1080x1080 or 1080x1350 \| High quality required \|

\| File Format \| JPG or PNG \|carousel must be uploaded as images \|

\| First Slide \| Must be bold hook \| This determines if people swipe
\|

\| Last Slide \| Must include CTA \| Ask for save, share, or follow \|

Carousel Structure That Works:

Slide 1 (Hook): Bold headline + visual that stops the scroll

Slides 2-8: One key point per slide, consistent design

Last Slide (CTA): \"Save this\", \"Share with a friend\", \"DM us\"

## **PART 3: POSTING BEST PRACTICES**

### **3.1 Posting Schedule**

Optimal Posting Times:

\- Morning: 6:00 AM - 9:00 AM CT

\- Midday: 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM CT

\- Evening: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM CT

Best Days:

\- Monday through Wednesday (strongest engagement)

\- Thursday and Friday (good)

\- Weekends (lower engagement, but less competition)

Posting Frequency:

\- Reels: 1+ per day minimum

\- Carousels/Posts: 3-5 per week

\- Stories: 3-5 per day (to stay top-of-mind)

### **3.2 Caption Best Practices**

Caption Structure:

Hook (first line - visible before \"more\")

\-

Body (value, context, story)

\-

CTA (what you want them to do)

\-

#hashtags (at the end)

Caption Rules:

\- First line must hook (visible before \"more\")

\- Can be long (up to 2,200 characters)

\- Use line breaks for readability

\- Include CTAs naturally

\- Put hashtags at END of caption (not first comment)

### **3.3 Hashtag Strategy**

Hashtag Rules:

\- Use 5-15 hashtags max

\- Put them at the END of caption (not in first comment---Instagram
changed this)

\- Mix 2-3 broad hashtags with 5-10 niche-specific ones

\- Never use 30 hashtags (signals spam)

Niche-Specific Hashtags for ThriveXDNA:

### **3.4 Link Strategy**

Instagram Link Rules:

\- Post captions: NO clickable links

\- Bio link: Use Linktree or single landing page

\- Story link sticker: Only clickable option in Stories

\- \"Link in bio\" CTA: Train audience to check bio

Bio Link Best Practices:

\- Update regularly to match current promotion

\- Use Linktree or similar for multiple links

\- Or use single focused landing page

## **PART 4: ENGAGEMENT RULES**

### **4.1 Comment Management**

Response Time Requirements:

\- First 2 hours: Critical (Instagram boosts posts with early
engagement)

\- Within 4 hours: Respond to all comments

\- Within 24 hours: Reply to every single comment

Why Early Replies Matter:

Instagram\'s algorithm prioritizes content with active engagement in the
first hours after posting. Quick responses encourage more comments,
triggering broader distribution.

Comment Response Guidelines:

\- Respond to questions with helpful answers

\- Ask follow-up questions to spark conversation

\- Thank viewers for specific compliments

\- Never argue with negative commenters---ignore or hide

### **4.2 Engagement Strategies**

Within Your Content:

\- Reels: End with question, use text overlays

\- Carousels: Last slide CTA for save/share

\- Stories: Use interactive stickers (polls, questions)

\- Posts: Ask questions in caption

Outside Your Content:

\- Reply to comments on your posts (first)

\- Comment on other relevant accounts daily

\- Engage with your niche community

\- Respond to DMs within 24 hours

### **4.3 Story Engagement**

Daily Story Strategy:

\- Post 3-5 Stories per day

\- At least 1 must be interactive (poll, question, quiz)

\- Use link sticker when promoting content

\- Add to Story Highlights for evergreen content

Interactive Story Features:

\- Poll stickers: \"Which do you prefer? A or B?\"

\- Question stickers: Get audience questions/feedback

\- Quiz stickers: Test knowledge

\- Countdown stickers: Build anticipation for launches

\- Link stickers: Drive traffic (requires 10K+ followers)

## **PART 7: VIDEO PRODUCTION WORKFLOW**

### **7.1 Creating Instagram Reels (Reference to Main Guide)**

For detailed video production steps, see
\`VIDEOPRODUCTIONCHECKLISTWITHWHY.md\`. Below is the abbreviated
workflow for Instagram Reels:

Phase 1: Research (15-30 min)

1\. Check Instagram\'s trending audio

2\. Identify trending formats in your niche

3\. Research what top creators are posting

4\. Validate your topic idea

Phase 2: Concept Development (15 min)

1\. Choose trending audio (strong recommendation)

2\. Design your hook (first 1 second)

3\. Plan the content structure

4\. Write script if needed (short, conversational)

Phase 3: Production (30-60 min)

1\. Record A-roll (on-camera, high energy)

2\. Record B-roll (supporting visuals)

3\. Record multiple takes

4\. Capture trending sound or voiceover

Phase 4: Editing (30-60 min)

1\. Cut to optimal length (15-30 seconds)

2\. Add captions (every word)

3\. Add text overlays synced to audio

4\. Use trending audio

5\. Add transitions and effects

6\. Export at 1080x1920

Phase 5: Upload & Optimize

1\. Write caption with strong hook

2\. Add 5-15 relevant hashtags at end

3\. Post at optimal times

### **7.2 Creating Carousels**

Carousel Creation Steps:

1\. Plan 5-10 slides with one point each

2\. Design each slide in Canva (consistent style)

3\. Create bold hook on first slide

4\. Add CTA on last slide (save, share, follow)

5\. Export all slides as individual images

6\. Upload as carousel in correct order

7\. Write caption with value and hashtags

### **7.3 Creating Stories**

Story Creation Steps:

1\. Open Instagram camera

2\. Choose content type (photo, video, Boomerang)

3\. Add text, stickers, or effects

4\. Use interactive stickers (polls, questions)

5\. Add link sticker if promoting content

6\. Post to Story

7\. Add to relevant Story Highlights
