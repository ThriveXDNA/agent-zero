## **PART 1: TWITTER/X ALGORITHM - UNDERSTANDING WHAT WORKS**

### **1.1 How Twitter/X\'s Algorithm Actually Works**

Twitter/X\'s algorithm prioritizes content based on engagement. The
platform rewards content that sparks conversation, gets shared, and
keeps users on the platform.

The Core Ranking Factors for Twitter/X (Ranked by Importance):

\| Priority \| Signal \| Why It Matters \|

\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|

\| 1 (Highest) \| Engagement (Replies) \| Replies are the strongest
signal. Content that sparks conversation gets massive reach. \|

\| 2 \| Retweets & Quotes \| Shares extend reach significantly. Quote
tweets add extra engagement. \|

\| 3 \| Likes \| Positive engagement signal but less important than
replies/shares. \|

\| 4 \| Impressions \| How many people saw the tweet initially. \|

\| 5 (Lowest) \| Clicks \| Clicks on links or media show intent but are
less weighted. \|

The Twitter/X Difference:

Twitter/X is a conversation platform, not a broadcast platform. The best
tweets:

\- Spark discussion and debate

\- Encourage replies

\- Are shareable

\- Are timely and relevant

### **1.2 Content Type Performance**

\| Content Type \| Reach \| Best For \|

\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|

\| Text Tweets \| Highest \| Thoughts, opinions, thread starters \|

\| Threads \| High \| Detailed content, storytelling \|

\| Images \| High \| Engagement, value-add \|

\| Videos \| High \| Tutorials, demonstrations \|

\| Polls \| High \| Engagement, audience research \|

### **2.2 Twitter Threads**

\| Specification \| Standard \| Notes \|

\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\--\|\-\-\-\-\-\--\|

\| Length \| 3-10 tweets \| More for detailed content \|

\| Structure \| Hook tweet + value tweets + CTA \| Tell a complete story
\|

Tweet 2-8: Step-by-step or point-by-point

Tweet 9 (CTA): \"Follow for more\" or \"Retweet to share\"

## **PART 4: ENGAGEMENT RULES**

### **4.1 Comment/Reply Management**

Response Time Requirements:

\- First 15-30 minutes: Critical (Twitter moves fast)

\- Within 1 hour: Respond to all replies

\- Within 24 hours: Reply to every interaction

Why Fast Replies Matter:

Twitter/X is fast-paced. Early engagement signals to the algorithm that
your content is worth showing to more people.

Reply Guidelines:

\- Respond to questions with helpful answers

\- Engage with criticism professionally

\- Thank engagement

\- Ask follow-up questions

### **4.2 Engagement Strategies**

Within Your Tweets:

\- End with questions

\- Ask for opinions

\- Create debate

\- Use polls

Outside Your Tweets:

\- Reply to other accounts daily (8-15 minimum)

\- Retweet relevant content

\- Quote tweet with added value

\- Engage with industry leaders

### **4.3 The 80/20 Rule on Twitter/X**

Content Breakdown:

\- 80%: Value tweets (educational, entertaining, insights)

\- 20%: Promotion (product mentions, links)

Never:

\- Post only promotional content

\- Drop links without adding value

\- Only retweet without original content

## **PART 7: CONTENT CREATION WORKFLOW**

### **7.1 Creating Tweets**

Tweet Creation Steps:

1\. Identify valuable insight or angle

2\. Write compelling hook (first sentence)

3\. Add value (tip, insight, story)

4\. End with question or CTA

5\. Add 1-2 hashtags max

6\. Post at optimal time

Tweet Formulas:

Formula 1: The Hot Take

\[Controversial statement\]\...

But here\'s why I believe this \[reason\]

What\'s your take? Drop it below 👇

Formula 2: The Quick Tip

\[Number\] things I learned about \[topic\]:

1\. \[Insight\]

2\. \[Insight\]

3\. \[Insight\]

What\'s your #1? 👇

Formula 3: The Question

I\'m curious about \[topic\]\...

\[Specific question\]

Drop your answers below 👇

Formula 4: The Thread Hook

I just \[achieved result\]. Here\'s exactly how (🧵):

\[First tweet hooks readers to follow thread\]

### **7.2 Creating Threads**

Thread Creation Steps:

1\. Choose a topic worth detailed coverage

2\. Write the hook tweet (most important)

3\. Plan 3-10 tweets in sequence

4\. Add value to each tweet

5\. End with clear CTA

6\. Post as thread

7\. Pin the first tweet

### **7.3 Creating Image Tweets**

Image Tweet Steps:

1\. Create image in Canva with key point

2\. Keep text minimal on image

3\. Add value in caption

4\. Include 1-2 hashtags

5\. Post with image

## **THE LINK WORKAROUND**

Critical Twitter/X Rule:

Links in tweet body = suppressed reach. Never post raw URLs.

Workarounds:

Option 1: Image Screenshot

\- Take screenshot of blog post

\- Post image with caption: \"Full breakdown → link in bio\"

Option 2: Quote + Image

\- Create text image with key points

\- Caption: \"Here\'s what this means for you\...\"

Option 3: Bio Link

\- Put link in bio

\- Tweet: \"Link in bio\"
